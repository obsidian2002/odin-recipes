Noodles Recipe

This is a egg ramen noodles

This is a traditional food of japan.Its main ingredients are egg ,meat ,noodles & spices & vegetable.


ingredients :
  • Egg
  • Meat
  • Nooodles
  • Vegetable

Steps needed making the dish :
  1. First boil water for 2-3 minutes
  2. Put noodles on boiled water
  3. Put boiled egg sliced in half in the foods
  4. Add meat & spices in the bown & enjoy !
